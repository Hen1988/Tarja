About Tarja

Tarja the dog was adopted on March 06, 2019 at the age of one.
She was found in the desert with two little puppies and was taken to the dog shelter.

Tarja was named after a Finnish female singer known as Tarja Turunen,
Tarja the dog began to make her own art and became even greater than the original singer.

Tarja started her own career from making noises in the living room, damending food and toys from her owner,
and quickly based her career as a top leader musician with fans from all over the world.
